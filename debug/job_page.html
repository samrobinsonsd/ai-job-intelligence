      We are a well-funded, early-stage startup hiring our Founding Solutions Engineering team to help build and own our enterprise customer motion from the ground up. This role is perfect for a hands-on, technically deep builder who thrives in fast-moving environments, enjoys making things work in the real world, and partners closely with founders, Product, and R&D to deploy and scale complex, AI-driven enterprise solutions.

      You will own the technical side of the post-sale customer journey, from deployment and integration through adoption, expansion, and ongoing success, while helping define how our delivery function evolves as we grow.

      Key Responsibilities

      Customer Onboarding & Process Analysis

      • Engage with customers post-close to understand workflows, operational processes, and deployment requirements.
      • Translate findings into clear implementation plans for AI-powered solutions.

      Data, AI Models & Workflow Development

      • Partner with customers to assess data readiness and gather inputs for AI workflows.
      • Configure and integrate AI components into tailored, AI-driven workflows.
      • Collaborate with R&D to close data gaps and extend product capabilities when needed.

      Deployment & Proof of Value

      • Own end-to-end solution deployment and ensure models meet performance and business value expectations.
      • Define success metrics and drive adoption post-launch.

      Integration, Security & Rollout

      • Lead integrations with enterprise systems (e.g., Finance, ERP, email platforms), ensuring scalability and security.
      • Guide customers through security, compliance, and IT approval processes.
      • Plan and execute rollout to drive smooth implementation and adoption.

      Qualifications

      • 7+ years of experience in Implementation Engineering, Solutions Engineering, Technical Consulting, Professional Services or a similar post-sale customer-facing role.
      • Strong analytical skills with the ability to translate business needs into technical solutions.
      • Strong understanding of AI concepts and their enterprise applications.
      • High adoption of modern AI tools and workflows.
      • Experience supporting deployments across cloud environments and enterprise system integrations.
      • Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.
      • Demonstrated ability to manage solution delivery across the full post-sale customer lifecycle.

      Nice to Have

      • Experience with process mining, workflow mapping, or operational analytics
      • Familiarity with enterprise platforms
      • Financial services industry experience

      Compensation

      The expected salary for this role is $180,000 to $230,000 OTE. This is a good-faith estimate but does not include equity or other forms of compensation. Pay will be based on experience level, and those outside the salary band are welcome to apply.
